Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Nagorno-Karabakh conflict
    • September 2022 Armenia–Azerbaijan clashes
      • Armenia formally triggers article 4 of the Collective Security Treaty Organization after accusing Azerbaijan of launching an invasion. Armenian Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan says "We asked support from CSTO, including military support to restore Armenia’s territorial integrity and ensure the withdrawal of the Azerbaijani Armed Forces from the territory of Armenia". (Armenpress)
  • Israeli–Palestinian conflict
    • Two Palestinian members of the al-Aqsa Martyrs' Brigade and an Israeli soldier are killed during a shootout at a checkpoint in Jenin, in the occupied West Bank. (Al Jazeera)
  • Aftermath of the 2021 Kyrgyzstan–Tajikistan clashes
    • Two Tajik border guards are killed and two others are wounded during clashes with Kyrgyz guards, who accuse Tajikistan of taking positions at a demarcated area. (Reuters)

Disasters and accidents

  • 2022 Pacific typhoon season
    • Typhoon Muifa makes landfall in eastern China with the maximum wind speed near its centre reaching 42 metres per second (151.2 km/h or 94 mph). (Bloomberg) (Reuters)
  • Twelve people are killed and many others are injured in a three vehicle collision in Kyaukpadaung, Myanmar. (Xinhua)
  • Eleven people are killed and 29 others are injured when a bus falls into a gorge in Poonch district, Jammu and Kashmir, India. (AP)

Law and crime

  • Iceland v Iceland Foods Ltd
    • The British supermarket chain Iceland launches an appeal to the EUIPO against the country of Iceland in a 20 year long legal process over the use and trademark of the name "Iceland". (Retail Gazette) (Grapevine) (City A.M.) (Retail Week)
  • Two Thai military personnel are killed and one is injured after a gunman opens fire at a military facility in Bangkok. The gunman is later arrested. (CNA)
