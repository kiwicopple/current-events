Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Russo-Ukrainian War
    • 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine
      • 2022 bombing of Odesa
        • Two Russian Kalibr cruise missiles hit Odesa's sea port less than a day after a deal to export grain from Ukraine was signed between Russia and Ukraine; initial reports indicate no related infrastructure damage. (AP)
      • A Russian cruise missile attack destroys a railway station in Kirovohrad Oblast, killing three people and injuring some others. (France 24)

Business and economy

  • Fitch Ratings downgrades Ukrainian debt outlook to "C", one grade above default, due to requests from Ukrainian authorities to postpone payments on bonds. (Bloomberg)

Disasters and accidents

  • Wildfires in 2022
    • 2022 European heat waves
      • 2022 European and Mediterranean wildfires
        • In Greece, nine people are rescued after wildfires burn in the Lesbos island and threaten the resort town of Vatera amidst the European heat wave. (Reuters) (The New York Post)
        • Wildfires continue to rage in southwestern France, prompting more people to evacuate their homes. (NBC News)
    • 2022 California wildfires
      • The Oak Fire in Mariposa County, California, U.S. grows to over 6,555 acres near Yosemite, with 10 homes destroyed. (Axios)
  • Flash floods kill at least 21 people after the Roudbal River overflowed in Estahban, Fars Province, Iran. 55 others were rescued and six others remain missing. (AP)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Japan
      • COVID-19 vaccination in Japan
        • Japan begins administering fourth COVID-19 vaccine doses for medical staff and elderly care workers. (Japan Today)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in the Republic of Ireland
      • COVID-19 vaccination in the Republic of Ireland
        • The Irish Ministry of Health announces changes to Ireland’s vaccination program including a second booster shot dose for people aged 50 and 64 and a first mRNA vaccine for people aged 5 and 11 who are immunocompromised. (TheJournal.ie) (gov.ie)
  • 2022 monkeypox outbreak
    • The monkeypox outbreak is declared a Public Health Emergency of International Concern by the World Health Organization. (The Guardian)

Law and crime

  • Aerolíneas Argentinas Flight 1882 makes an emergency landing in Comodoro Rivadavia, Chubut Province, after receiving a bomb threat. The flight with 169 passengers and six crew members had departed Aeroparque Jorge Newbery in Buenos Aires bound for Ushuaia, Tierra del Fuego Province. (Infobae)

Politics and elections

  • 2022 Vanuatuan presidential election
    • Nikenike Vurobaravu is elected President of Vanuatu by the country's Electoral College in the eighth round of voting. Vurobaravu will succeed outgoing President Tallis Obed Moses. (Radio New Zealand Pacific)
  • Government of Thailand
    • Prime Minister Prayut Chan-o-cha survives a no-confidence vote with 256 confidence votes, 206 against and nine abstaining. (AP)
