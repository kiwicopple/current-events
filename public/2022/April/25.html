Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Russo-Ukrainian War
    • 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine
      • April 2022 Belgorod and Bryansk attacks
        • Two large fires break out at an oil refinery and a military depot in Bryansk, Russia. (The Guardian)
      • Russian occupation of Kherson Oblast
        • Ihor Kolykhaiev, mayor of Kherson, announces that Russian forces have taken control of the Kherson City Council. (Ukrinform)
      • Russian missile strikes hit numerous train stations across Ukraine. (The Guardian)
      • A person is killed after Russia bombs the Kremenchuk Oil Refinery in Kremenchuk, Ukraine. (Reuters) (Ukrinform)

  • 2022 Tiraspol bombing
    • The office of the Transnistrian Ministry of State Security is attacked with grenade launchers by unknown assailants; there is damage reported to the building, but no reports of casualties. (Reuters)
  • List of rocket attacks from Lebanon on Israel
    • A rocket is fired from Lebanon into Matzuva, Israel; Israel responds by firing at targets in Lebanon. (Times of Israel)
