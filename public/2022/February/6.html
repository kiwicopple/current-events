  • 2021–2022 Russo-Ukrainian crisis
    • Russia–NATO relations
      • French President Emmanuel Macron says a deal to "avoid war in Ukraine" is "within reach" and says that it is legitimate for Russia to raise its own security concerns to NATO. Macron is due to speak to Vladimir Putin in Moscow tomorrow. (BBC News)
  • Afghanistan–Pakistan border skirmishes
    • Five Pakistani troops are killed after "militants from inside Afghanistan" opened fire on a border post in Kurram District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. The army says it retaliated, causing heavy casualties among the militants. The Taliban government denies the firing had come from within Afghan territory. (Reuters)
