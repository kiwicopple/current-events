Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Syrian civil war
    • October 2022 northern Aleppo clashes
      • The jihadist group Tahrir al-Sham (HTS) captures the city of Afrin in Aleppo Governorate, Syria, following days of clashes with the Turkish-backed Syrian National Army, according to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights. (Voice of America)
      • HTS forces capture the town of Jindires amid ongoing clashes in the region. (Al Mayadeen English)
    • Eighteen soldiers are killed and 20 more injured as a bomb explodes on a military bus near Damascus. (Reuters)
  • Israeli–Palestinian conflict
    • Rival Palestinian factions signed an agreement in Algiers aimed at resolving 15 years of discord. Participants in the talks included Azzam al-Ahmad, head of the Fatah delegation, Ismael Haniyeh, head of the Islamist Hamas movement, and leaders or representatives of fourteen other factions. (Al Jazeera)
