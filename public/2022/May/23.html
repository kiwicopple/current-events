    • 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine

Disasters and accidents

  • Authorities say that 300 people have been displaced by recent flooding in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. (AP)
  • Seven people are killed and 23 others are injured after a high-speed craft carrying 134 people catches fire off the coast of Real, Quezon, Philippines. (Sky News)
  • Six people are killed when a building collapses in Abadan, Iran. (AP)
  • A gas cylinder catches fire and explodes at a restaurant in the Abu Dhabi area of Khalidiyah Mall, United Arab Emirates, killing two people and wounding 120 more. (Reuters)
  • A mudflow hits the village of Ak-Suu in the Aksy District in Kyrgyzstan, leaving it in isolation and three cars trapped. (AKIPress)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in India
        • COVID-19 pandemic in Telangana
          • Telangana reports its first case of the Omicron BA.5 variant in an 80-year old with no history of travel. (The Indian Express)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in mainland China
        • COVID-19 pandemic in Beijing
          • Beijing extends its stay-at-home order for workers and students as well as ordering mass COVID-19 testing amidst a rise of COVID-19 cases in the area. (ABC News)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Spain
      • Emeritus Queen consort Sofía of Spain tests positive for COVID-19 after visiting Miami, United States. (Diez Minutos)
    • COVID-19 vaccine
      • Pfizer and BioNTech say that their COVID-19 vaccine is 80% effective in young kids. (The Washington Post)
  • 2022 monkeypox outbreak
    • Scotland confirms its first case of monkeypox. The person is being treated in a hospital. Following the reports of the first case, smallpox vaccines are stockpiled in the country. (Reuters) (BBC News) (Glasgow Times)
    • Denmark confirms its first case of monkeypox. (Reuters)
    • England discovers 36 more monkeypox cases bringing the total to 56 in the country. (Reuters)
    • The U.S. CDC says that American health officials are in the process of releasing Jynneos vaccines to combat monkeypox cases. (Axios)
    • Pakistan is on high alert of monkeypox as cases rise in the United Kingdom. (The News Pakistan)
    • Morocco has three new suspected cases of monkeypox. (English News)
  • Polio eradication, Polio in Pakistan
    • Pakistan launches a new anti-polio drive after a third case of the disease is found in the country. (The Register Citizen)
  • In Australia, Queensland Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk announces that Queensland residents will have access to influenza vaccines across the state amidst a rise of influenza A cases after two years of low cases due to COVID-19. (Seven News)

International relations

  • Cross-Strait relations, China-United States relations
    • President of the United States Joe Biden states that American troops would defend Taiwan militarily in the event of a Chinese invasion, a departure from a traditional policy of strategic ambiguity. (The New York Times)

    • In the first trial for war crimes during the Russian invasion of Ukraine, 21-year-old Vadim Shishimarin, a Russian soldier, is sentenced to life in prison for the murder of a 62-year-old civilian. (CNN)
