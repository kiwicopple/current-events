Disasters and accidents

  • Twenty-seven people are injured after a bus rolls over near Kingsville, Maryland, United States. (AP)
  • Four people are killed and 16 others are injured in a traffic collision in San Juan Chamula, Chiapas, Mexico. (AP)
  • The Russian-appointed mayor of Enerhodar is severely injured in an explosion in the Ukrainian city of Enerhodar, Russian-occupied Zaporizhzhia Oblast. The cause of the blast is unclear. (Reuters)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic

      • Oman announces that it will repeal all COVID-19 restrictions, including its mask mandate. (AP)
  • 2022 monkeypox outbreak
    • Austria confirms its first case of monkeypox. (Reuters)

Law and crime

  • An Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps colonel is assassinated in Tehran, Iran, by unknown gunmen riding on motorcycles. (AP)
  • Four people are killed after a failed attempt to smuggle drugs to Jordan from Syria. (Reuters)

Sports

  • 2021–22 Serie A
    • A.C. Milan wins the scudetto of the Serie A, the highest level of Italian league football, after finishing the season with 86 points, two points over the runners-up and their intercity rivals, Inter Milan. Milan wins their 19th Serie A title overall and their first title since the 2010–11 season. (CNA)
  • 2021–22 Premier League
    • Manchester City wins their second consecutive Premier League title, their fourth in 5 years, and their sixth overall in the final day of the season following a 3–2 comeback against Aston Villa. Manchester City wins the league with 93 points, one point over Liverpool. This is the first time since 2019 that the title has been decided on the final day of the season. (The New York Times)
