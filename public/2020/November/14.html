Armed conflicts and attacks
  • Tigray conflict
    • Asmara rocket attacks
      • Explosions are reported in the Eritrean capital Asmara amid reports of a missile attack from Tigray. The Eritrean government confirms missiles were launched at the capital but denied the city had been hit, saying that they landed in the countryside. The attack comes just hours after the TPLF threatened to target Eritrea. (Eritrea Hub)
    • Tigray People's Liberation Front forces fire missiles at Gondar Airport and Bahir Dar Airport in Amhara Region, Ethiopia. A spokesman for the TPLF says "As long as the attacks on the people of Tigray do not stop, the attacks will intensify". (CNN)
    • Hundreds of people cross the Tekezé River on small boats into Sudan, fleeing the escalating fighting in Tigray. There are now around 8,000 refugees stranded on the Ethiopia–Sudan border. (Reuters)
  • 2020 Western Sahara clashes
    • The Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic declares war on Morocco. (Axios)

Disasters and accidents
  • 2020 Pacific typhoon season
    • Around 468,000 people are to be evacuated in Vietnam ahead of the landfall of Typhoon Vamco, which has already killed 53 people in the Philippines. Prime Minister Nguyễn Xuân Phúc warns people of the seriousness of the storm. (Reuters)
  • 2020 Atlantic hurricane season
    • Tropical Storm Iota strenghtens in the Caribbean Sea and is expected to intensify in the coming hours, threatening many parts of Central America, including Belize, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama and Colombia in South America. The storm is expected to grow into a hurricane. (CNN)
  • Piatra Neamț hospital fire
    • A fire breaks out in a hospital in Piatra Neamț, Romania, killing at least 10 people. (Digi24)

Health and environment
  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Europe
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Austria
        • Chancellor Sebastian Kurz announces a two-week national lockdown effective November 17. All schools, non-essential services and shops will close, and people will be told not to meet anyone from outside their household as cases rise. (BBC)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Greece
        • Education Minister Niki Kerameus announces the closure of kindergartens, primary schools, and childcare centers across the country for two weeks from November 16 as part of tightened lockdown measures aimed to contain the spread of COVID-19. (Ekathimerini)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Poland
        • Poland reports 548 new fatalities, the highest single-day figure in the country since the pandemic began and pushes the nationwide death toll above 10,000. (RMF 24)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Russia
        • Russia reports a record 22,702 new cases in the past 24 hours, pushing the nationwide total since the start of the pandemic to 1.9 million cases. (The Moscow Times)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Ukraine
        • The number of new confirmed cases in Ukraine surges to a record of 12,524 cases, pushing the nationwide total since the start of the pandemic to 525,176 cases. (Kyiv Post)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Iran
        • The government announces that Iran will impose stricter restrictions from November 21. In Tehran and about 100 other towns and cities which are classified as "red zone" or "Level 3", non-essential businesses will close and cars will not be allowed to leave or enter the city. (IRNA) (Reuters)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Japan
        • Japan reports 1,731 new confirmed cases, a new record for three days in row since the pandemic began as many prefectures like Osaka and Chiba reported a record number of daily cases. (Kyodo News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in South Korea
        • South Korea reports 205 new confirmed cases as of midnight, exceeding 200 for the first time since September, according to data from Korea Disease Control and Prevention Agency (KDCA). (Yonhap News Agency)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Canada
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Ontario
        • Ontario reports 1,581 new infections, bringing the caseload total in the province to 92,761 cases and marks the highest single-day figure of new cases since the beginning of the pandemic. (Global News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Quebec
        • Quebec reports a new single-day record of 1,448 cases, bringing the total in the province to 122,634 cases. (Montreal Gazette)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Saskatchewan
        • Saskatchewan reports 308 new cases, a new single-day increase record since the pandemic. This record cases brings the total to 1,691 cases. (CKCK-DT)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Australia, Travel restrictions related to the COVID-19 pandemic
      • Western Australia reopens its hard border for the first time in seven months after the closure due to restrictions related to the COVID-19 pandemic. (ABC Australia)
