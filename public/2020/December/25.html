Disasters and accidents

  • A vessel overturns and goes under water due to strong winds in Lake Albert on the Ugandan side, leaving 26 people dead. (BBC)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Hong Kong
        • The authorities extends the compulsory quarantine in designed hotels to 21 days for visitors outside China, Macau, and Taiwan starting tomorrow and bans all people who stayed in South Africa over the past 21 days to enter Hong Kong in order to prevent the spread of new variants of SARS-CoV-2. (The Straits Times)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Indonesia
        • Indonesia reports a record 258 new deaths and 6,324 new recoveries in the past 24 hours, thus bringing the nationwide totals to 20,847 deaths and 570,304 recoveries. (CNN Indonesia)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Japan
        • Japan reports the first case of new variant of SARS-COV-2 from five people that arrived from the United Kingdom. (The Japan Times)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Lebanon, Variant of Concern 202012/01
        • Lebanon reports the first case of a new variant of SARS-CoV-2 from a passenger on an incoming Middle East Airlines flight 202 from London on December 21. (The National)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in South Korea
        • The number of new confirmed cases in South Korea reaches a record 1,241 cases in the past 24 hours, bringing the nationwide total to 54,770 cases. (Yonhap News Agency)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Saudi Arabia, COVID-19 vaccine
        • Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman receives the vaccine. (The Hill)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Kyrgyzstan
        • Kyrgyzstan exceeds 80,000 cases of COVID-19. (Xinhua News Agency)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Europe
      • COVID-19 pandemic in France, Variant of Concern 202012/01
        • France reports the first case of new variant of SARS-CoV-2 in Tours following a French citizens who lives in England and left London on December 19 tested positive for COVID-19. (Sky News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Russia, Variant of Concern 202012/01
        • Rospotrebnadzor orders the 14-day quarantine for anyone arrived in the country from the United Kingdom with exception of aircraft crew members starting tomorrow due to spread of a more contagious variant of SARS-CoV-2. (The Moscow Times)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Turkey
        • Turkey signs a deal with BioNTech to secure up to 30 million doses of vaccine. (Daily Sabah)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Africa
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Morocco, COVID-19 vaccine
        • Morocco is expected to acquire 65 million doses of the vaccine from AstraZeneca and Sinopharm. (ABC News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Mali, COVID-19 pandemic in France
        • Malian opposition leader Soumaila Cissé dies from COVID-19 in France two months after he was released by jihadist militants in a prisoner swap in Mali. (France 24)

Law and crime

  • 2020 Nashville explosion
    • A "significant" explosion occurs in downtown Nashville, Tennessee, U.S., leaving behind structural damage and three people injured. Police believe the explosion, which has been linked to an RV, was an "intentional act". (CNN) (The Tennessean)
    • The explosion results in AT&T service outages across the U.S. due to infrastructure damage to a service facility located near the blast site. Cellular, wireline telephone, Internet, and U-verse television service, as well as multiple local 9-1-1 and non-emergency phone networks, are among the services affected. (WKRN News 2) (The Tennessean)
  • A court in Russia sentences prominent historian Oleg Sokolov to 12 years and a half in prison for the murder and dismemberment of his student lover. Sokolov, a former history professor at St. Petersburg State University, pled guilty to the murder. (Reuters)
