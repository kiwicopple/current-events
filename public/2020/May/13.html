Armed conflicts and attacks
  • Israeli–Palestinian conflict
    • Israeli troops kill a 15-year-old Palestinian boy and wound four others at a refugee camp near Hebron, where they are looking for the rock thrower who killed a soldier yesterday. (Ynetnews)

Health and environment
  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Poland
      • Poland extends its ban on foreigners entering the country until June 12 due to COVID-19. (Reuters)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Lesotho
      • Lesotho reports its first case. The case is a person who returned from the Middle East and is asymptomatic. (Guardian)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Chile
      • Due to a spike in cases, the government announces a total lockdown in Santiago. (The New York Times)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in the United States
      • COVID-19 pandemic in New York
        • COVID-19 pandemic in New York City
          • New York City goes 58 consecutive days without a fatal pedestrian traffic collision, the most since tracking began in 1983. (ABC News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Maryland
        • Maryland Governor Larry Hogan announces that the stay-at-home order will be lifted on May 15, except for Prince George's and Montgomery counties. Groups of over ten will remain forbidden. (The Hill)

Politics and elections
  • 2020 California's 25th congressional district special election
    • Democrat Christy Smith concedes defeat to Republican Mike Garcia in yesterday's special election for California's 25th congressional district after vote tallying showed Garcia in the lead. The results it the first time since 1998 that Republicans flipped a Democratic-held House seat in California. (CNN) (The Hill)
  • Tom Thabane announces he is stepping down today as Lesotho's Prime Minister because murder accusations hamper his government. (Reuters)

Sports
  • Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on sports
    • 2020 AFL season
      • Plans of restarting the 2020 AFL season on June 11 have been halted when the South Australian government announced they would not allow the Adelaide and Port Adelaide football clubs to be exempt from the 14-day mandatory quarantine. Western Australian-based teams Fremantle and West Coast have plans to temporary relocate to Gold Coast, Queensland. (New.com.au)
