Armed conflicts and attacks
  • Korean conflict
    • North and South Korean soldiers exchange gunfire across the DMZ at a South Korean guard post, but no injuries are reported. (Reuters)
  • Sinai insurgency
    • Egypt's military says that 15 of its members were killed or wounded in recent operations in Sinai and that 126 suspected militants had been killed. (Reuters)
  • Venezuelan Interior Minister Nestor Reverol says the country's security forces have foiled a "marine incursion" by "terrorist mercenaries" from Colombia, killing at least eight gunmen and capturing two others. The foreign group reportedly landed on a beach in the city of La Guaira on speedboats. (Al Jazeera)

Business and economy
  • Indonesian e-commerce site Tokopedia launches a security investigation after a hacker said they stole the information of 91 million users and plans to release it on the dark web. (Reuters)

Health and environment
  • 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic
    • 2020 coronavirus pandemic in Russia
      • Russia reports a record 10,633 new cases of COVID-19 and 58 more deaths from the virus in the last 24 hours, bringing the country's total cases to 134,686 and 1,280 deaths. Moscow's mayor Sergei Sobyanin says the peak has still not been reached, warning residents to remain indoors. (BBC)
