Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Somali Civil War
    • A roadside bomb explodes in Dusmareb, Somalia, killing 12 agents working for the National Intelligence and Security Agency. The local head of the intelligence agency is among those killed. (Al Jazeera)
  • 2020–21 Sudanese–Ethiopian clashes
    • Finnish Foreign Minister Pekka Haavisto arrives in Khartoum to help cool down tensions between Sudan and Ethiopia. He will also visit a refugee camp containing refugees fleeing the Tigray War and visit Ethiopia after spending time in Sudan. (European Union)

Disasters and accidents

  • 2021 Uttarakhand glacier burst
    • A glacier breaks off in Joshimath, India, in Uttarakhand's Chamoli district resulting in flash floods along the Alaknanda River and Dhauliganga River. Nine people are found dead and 140 more are feared dead. (AP)
  • An earthquake of 6.1 magnitude occurs in the southern province of Davao del Sur, Philippines, with damage and aftershocks expected. No casualties have been reported. (PhilStar)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Afghanistan
        • Afghanistan receives 500,000 doses of Oxford-AstraZeneca vaccine from India. (The Hindu)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Bangladesh
        • Bangladesh begins a nationwide vaccination programme against COVID-19 in Dhaka. (All India Radio)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Cambodia
        • Cambodia receives the first 600,000 doses of Sinopharm vaccine from China. (ABC News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Vietnam
        • Vietnam surpasses 2,000 cases of COVID-19. (Reuters)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Canada
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Ontario
        • Ontario reports Canada's first case of Lineage P.1 variant in an Toronto resident who travelled from Brazil. (CP24)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Europe
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Austria
        • Austria tightens its border controls to all neighbouring countries to avoid all non-essential travel due to the pandemic. (The Local Austria)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Hungary
        • Hungary approves the use of Sputnik V vaccine in the country. (The Moscow Times)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in South Africa
      • South Africa suspends the rollout of Oxford-AstraZenenca's vaccine after the research finds it was less effective against a 501.V2 variant discovered in the country. (Sky News)
    • COVID-19 vaccine
      • Oxford–AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine
        • A limited-size clinical trial in Johannesburg suggests the Oxford–AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine is weak against the 501.V2 variant of SARS-CoV-2. (The Wall Street Journal)

International relations

  • Iran–United States relations after 1979
    • Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei says that the United States should "lift all sanctions" if it wants to reverse its nuclear steps, saying that Iran complied with all obligations of the 2015 deal and accusing the United States and European countries of not doing so. (Reuters)

Politics and elections

  • 2021 Ecuadorian general election
    • General elections occur in Ecuador to elect the successor of President Lenín Moreno. Socialist and former aide of President Rafael Correa, Andrés Arauz, is expected to win, but may face a run-off with centre-right candidate Guillermo Lasso. (DW)
  • 2021 Liechtenstein general election
    • General elections occur in Liechtenstein to elect the 25 members of the Landtag. (Euronews)
  • 2021 Myanmar protests, aftermath of the 2021 Myanmar coup d'état
    • Tens of thousands protest against the military coup in Myanmar's largest city Yangon, calling for the release of jailed leader Aung San Suu Kyi. The protests are the largest to occur in the country since the 2007 Saffron Revolution. (Reuters)

Sports

  • 2020 NFL season, Super Bowl LV
    • Sarah Thomas becomes the first female NFL official to officiate a Super Bowl. (The New York Times)
