Armed conflicts and attacks

  • 2021 Myanmar coup d'état
    • The military hands the power to Min Aung Hlaing and announces a state of emergency for a year, after detaining Aung San Suu Kyi and other leaders of the National League for Democracy (NLD) party. (BBC)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Japan
        • Tokyo surpasses 100,000 cases of COVID-19. (The Mainichi)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Pakistan
        • Pakistan receives 500,000 doses of Sinopharm's COVID-19 vaccine. (The News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Vietnam
        • Vietnam closes schools in 22 provinces ahead of the Tết holiday and ends a Communist Party congress early following the detection of a new cluster of COVID-19 cases in northern areas. (CNA)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Europe
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Armenia
        • Armenia approves the use of Sputnik V vaccine developed by Russia in the country. (Public Radio of Armenia)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Austria
        • Austria announces the easing of lockdown from February 8, allowing non-essential shops and schools to reopen while border restrictions will toughen and a curfew between 8:00 pm to 6:00 am local time will be in place. (Vienna.at)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Isle of Man
        • Isle of Man lifts their second "circuit-breaker" lockdown after 20 days without community case. (BBC)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Italy
        • Italy eases their national restrictions by allowing the reopening of bars, restaurants and museums in 16 "Yellow-zone" regions. But curfew from 10:00 pm to 5:00 am remains in place. (France 24)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Lithuania
        • Lithuania reports the first case of UK variant of SARS-CoV-2 in a woman from Vilnius who tested positive for COVID-19 in early January. (LRT)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Canada
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Ontario
        • Ontario reports the first case of 501.V2 variant in a person in Peel Region with no travel history. (CP24)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in South Africa
      • South Africa receives one million doses of Oxford-AstraZeneca's vaccine produced by Serum Institute of India at O.R. Tambo International Airport. (ABC News)

International relations

  • Myanmar–United States relations, 2021 Myanmar coup d'état
    • U.S. President Joe Biden threatens to re-impose sanctions on Myanmar's military leaders and calls for them to relinquish the power they seized in a coup. (Reuters)
  • Venezuelan presidential crisis
    • Andorra stops recognizing Juan Guaidó as interim president of Venezuela. (Diari d'Andorra)

Law and crime

  • Aftermath of the 2021 storming of the United States Capitol
    • Over 228 people have been charged or arrested since the storming of the United States Capitol. (Business Insider)
