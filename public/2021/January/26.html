Armed conflicts and attacks

  • Israeli–Palestinian conflict
    • An Israeli commander shoots and kills a Palestinian man who attempted to stab soldiers at a road junction near Nablus in the West Bank. (Reuters)

Disasters and accidents

  • Tornadoes of 2021
    • List of United States tornadoes in January 2021
      • An EF3 tornado hits Fultondale, Alabama, leaving one person dead and 30 others injured. (AP), (National Weather Service Tweet)

Health and environment

  • COVID-19 pandemic
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Asia
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Indonesia
        • Indonesia surpasses one million cases of COVID-19. In addition, a record 10,868 recoveries are reported in the past 24 hours. (detikHealth)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Iran
        • Iran approves Sputnik V vaccine for use in the country. (Ynet News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Thailand
        • Thailand reports a record of 959 newly confirmed cases in the past 24 hours, including 914 cases in Samut Sakhon reported yesterday evening, bringing the nationwide total of confirmed cases to 14,646. (Bangkok Post)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in the United Arab Emirates
        • The United Arab Emirates reports a record 3,601 new confirmed cases in the past 24 hours, bringing the nationwide total of confirmed cases to 285,147. (Arab News)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Uzbekistan
        • Uzbekistan lifts their restrictions aimed to combat the spread of COVID-19 and announces that concert halls will be allowed to resume. However, visitors will still be required not to exceed past 50% in capacity, and visitors and staff will be required to wear masks. (Trend)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Europe
      • COVID-19 pandemic in the Republic of Ireland
        • The government extends the Level 5 lockdown restrictions until March 5. It also agree to impose 14-day mandatory hotel quarantine for people who travelled to the Republic of Ireland without negative COVID-19 test results as well as arrivals from Brazil and South Africa. (RTÉ)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in Portugal
        • Portugal reports a record 291 deaths in the past 24 hours, bringing the nationwide death toll to 11,012. (Expresso)
      • COVID-19 pandemic in the United Kingdom
        • The United Kingdom surpasses 100,000 deaths from COVID-19, becoming the fifth country to do so after the United States, Brazil, India, and Mexico. (BBC)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in Colombia
      • The Minister of National Defense of Colombia, Carlos Holmes Trujillo, dies from COVID-19. (Yahoo News)
    • COVID-19 pandemic in the United States
      • President Joe Biden orders 200 million doses of the COVID-19 vaccine to be distributed by the end of August. (NBC News)
    • The number of confirmed COVID-19 cases worldwide surpasses 100 million. (NBC News)
  • An outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza is reported in two backyard hobby grey crowned cranes in Central Italy. (Reuters)

Law and crime

  • Aftermath of the 2021 storming of the United States Capitol
    • Acting US Attorney of the District Columbia Michael R. Sherwin announces that 400 suspects have been identified and 135 people have been charged in connection to the storming of the United States Capitol. (ABC News)

Politics and elections

  • 2021 Italian government crisis
    • Prime Minister Giuseppe Conte resigns amid a political crisis over the government's handling of the COVID-19 pandemic. (BBC)
  • Cabinet of Joe Biden
    • The United States Senate votes 78-22 to confirm Antony Blinken as 71st Secretary of State. (AP)
